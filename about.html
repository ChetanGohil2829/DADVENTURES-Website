About DADVENTURES

Our Mission

DADVENTURES is a community charity founded in 2025. Our mission is to support dads and children by creating opportunities to spend meaningful time together.

Why Father–Child Bonding Matters

In the UK, around 1 in 5 dependent children grow up without a father figure at home. Studies show that when fathers are actively involved in childcare and play, children develop stronger social skills, emotional resilience, and perform better academically. Fathers benefit too — with better mental health and stronger family connections.

At DADVENTURES, we aim to change this.

  • Organise outdoor adventures and community events.
  • Provide a network of support for dads.
  • Invest back into the communities we live in.
